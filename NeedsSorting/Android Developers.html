Design for Android

Android users expect your app to look and behave in a way that's consistent with the platform. Not only should you follow material design guidelines for visual and navigation patterns, but you should also follow quality guidelines for compatibility, performance, security, and more.

The following links provide everything you need to design a high quality Android app.

Android app quality for cars

Android apps for cars should avoid driver distraction above all else. You can minimize distractions by following best practices, such as using voice commands and a very simple visual design. Great car experiences are predictive and predictable.
